About

I
'm an Digital Artist and Developer currently living in Asheville, NC. I specialize in imaginative graphic branding and illustration, unique but functionally tested front-end web and UI/UX design, and 3D modeling and animation.

Six years ago I was going pursue Art History (insert cute, scoffing emoji) - and today my professional accomplishments include:

  • Pilot creation of a 3D children's show
  • Prototyping an IOT authoring SaaS startup
  • Illustration and animation for a mobile Unity2D game
  • Design of hundreds of image ads, landing pages, responsive html emails for over 40 high-end national real estate communities
  • Development of personal portfolio sites for esteemed artists, thinkers, and colleagues
  • Google Analytics certification, extensive A/B testing and user behaviour analysis, and CRM database integration/management

Personal

I'm a North Florida migrant to Asheville, North Carolina for education at the University of North Carolina at Asheville, and a graduate of the New Media program specializing in 3D Animator under the tutelage esteemed industry professionals and trusted friends such as Christopher Oakley and Chris McKee.

My foundation is clean, but not stifled graphic design, my training is in classic 3D animation and modeling, and my most recent professionnal experience (NOTE) supplements with an extensive analysis and implementation of user-friendly, scalable, and to the point web-design and development.

Website Abstract (preaching, venting, perchance insightful?)

I have strong opinions on web design; well design in general, but as I spend have been spending most of my time in web-dev this site comes as a reaction to my day trade per-se.

The world really doesn't need another full-width slideshow, a web-page can be considered complete w/o a call-to-action above the fold. For that matter the term 'above-the-fold' is dead, no not dead, it was truly always irrelevant. As is 'mobile-first' in my mind, but I'll reserve that for a talk in person.

This site is an exercise in creativity, a bit of a personal challenge, a chance to write some actual code and not just throw some stock photos in a wordpress plugin, on a template filled with keywords strung together with prepositions. As well is it my throw at a new style/break/fresh-start, a chance to plan a creative and memorable user experience, contribute something memorable and entertaining to the web, and offer my work in a unique light - a bit of an unconvential portfolio environment.

I'll snip the manefesto (truly before it's time) and leave you to explore. I'd be happy and grateful to receive notifications of any bugs you encounter, general networking/professional introductions, contract inquiries of any size, or the like at my email address - ngpfontaine@gmail.com. Thank you, and have a good time during your stay.
