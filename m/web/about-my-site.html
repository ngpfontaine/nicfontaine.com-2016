About My Website

The initial objective was absolutely desktop first ("oh no he didn't..."). Full site-wise, I had a few objectives: I love using a keyboard; and have minimized reliance on mouse input for navigation almost as much as possible within browsers. So one goal was to give the user (1) a site they could navigate without moving their hands from the keyboard, while (2) challenging web-navigation conventions to give said user a unique and memorable experience. I encourage you to check it out.

Mobile-wise, and after fully designing the desktop site, I strove for something quick, simple, lightweight, with high usability. I broke from the weighty, but necessary single-page design of the full site and gave every section it's own page with a single image file, repetive navigation, while retaining full site controls throughout user location. Navigation was placed for optimal finger placement & movement, and content/weight stripped to its absolute minimum. A 'm.' subdomain was chosen to keep everything seperate from the size of the main site, while canonicalizing every page back to the main domain.
