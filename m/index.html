I
'm an Digital Artist and Developer currently living and working in San Francisco, CA. I specialize in fun & expressive 3D character animation and modeling, imaginative graphic branding and illustration, and unique but functionally tested front-end web and UI/UX design.

Six years ago I was going pursue Art History (insert cute, scoffing emoji) - and today my professional accomplishments include:

  • Pilot creation of a 3D children's show
  • Prototyping an IOT authoring SaaS startup
  • Illustration and animation for a mobile Unity2D game
  • Design of hundreds of image ads, landing pages, responsive html emails for over 40 high-end national real estate communities
  • Development of personal portfolio sites for esteemed artists, thinkers, and colleagues
  • Google Analytics certification, extensive A/B testing and user behaviour analysis, and CRM database integration/management

Personal

I was originally a North Florida migrant to Asheville, North Carolina for education at the University of North Carolina at Asheville, and a graduate of the New Media program specializing in 3D Animation under the tutelage esteemed industry professionals and trusted friends such as Christopher Oakley and Chris McKee.

My foundation is clean, but not stifled graphic design, my training is in classic 3D animation and modeling, and my most recent professionnal experience supplements with an extensive analysis and implementation of user-friendly, scalable, and to the point web-design and development.

Please enjoy my website (while I do love the mobile design, I encourage you to check out the unique experience of the desktop site), and feel free to contact me for professional consultation or just a friendly introduction.
