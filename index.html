About

I
'm an Digital Artist and Developer currently living in San Francisco, CA. I specialize in imaginative graphic branding and illustration, unique but functionally tested front-end web and UI/UX design, and 3D modeling and animation.

Seven years ago I was going pursue Art History (insert cute, scoffing emoji) - and today my professional accomplishments include:

  • Pilot creation of an educational 3D children's show
  • Illustration, storyboarding, & animation for a cross-platform Unity2D game
  • Design, development, and testing of a robust diagnostic tool to validate & catalog client systems for connection capabilities follow-up customer support
  • Design of hundreds of high-performing image ads, landing pages, responsive html emails for over 40 high-end national real estate communities
  • Prototype of an IOT authoring SaaS webapp
  • Leading animation research and execution spanning elementary, corporate, special-education, hospitality, (and more) settings
  • Google Analytics certification, extensive A/B testing and user behaviour analysis, and CRM database integration/management

Personal

I'm a North Florida migrant to Asheville, North Carolina for education at the University of North Carolina at Asheville, and a graduate of the comprehensive New Media program specializing in 3D Animation under the tutelage esteemed industry professionals and trusted friends Christopher Oakley and Chris McKee.

My foundation is clean design that transcends rote contemporary derivation, my training is in the classic storytelling of animation and modeling, and experience spans the journey of the birth of marketing campaigns to the testing, delivery, and presentation of production-ready software.

Website Abstract (preaching, venting, perchance insightful?)

I have strong convictions on design and user-experience. I want to challenge conventions, push my technological base, and identify personally with every project on which I work. In my free time I prefer to prod the potential of my capabilites applied to new and old technologies. One of my favourite themes is exploration; while giving the user enough information to engage with a product, allow them to modify and sometimes break to fit their unique needs. That's why I love open-source and libre software, testing, analytics, and support in place of crafting a singular, presentational product.

This site is an exercise in creativity, a bit of a personal challenge, a chance to write some actual code and not just throw some stock photos in a wordpress plugin, on a template filled with keywords strung together with prepositions. As well is it my throw at a new style/break/fresh-start, a chance to plan a creative and memorable user experience, contribute something memorable and entertaining to the web, and offer my work in a unique light - a bit of an unconvential portfolio environment.

I'll snip the manefesto (truly before it's time) and leave you to explore. I'd be happy and grateful to receive notifications of any bugs you encounter, general networking/professional introductions, contract inquiries of any size, or the like at my email address - ngpfontaine@gmail.com. Thank you, and have a good time during your stay.
